Classic Pasta Carbonara

A traditional Italian pasta dish with eggs, cheese, and pancetta. This authentic recipe creates a creamy sauce without using cream, relying on the technique of tempering eggs with hot pasta water.

15 min
Prep Time
20 min
Cook Time
35 min
Total Time
4
Servings
Medium
Difficulty

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti 400 g
  • Pancetta 150 g
  • Eggs 3 pieces
  • Parmesan Cheese 100 g
  • Black Pepper 1 tsp
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Meanwhile, cut pancetta into small cubes and cook in a large pan over medium heat until crispy and golden, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated parmesan cheese, and freshly ground black pepper until well combined.
  4. Drain pasta and immediately add to the pan with pancetta. Remove the pan from heat.
  5. Quickly stir in the egg mixture while tossing the pasta vigorously. Add reserved pasta water gradually until you achieve a creamy sauce consistency.
  6. Serve immediately in warm bowls, garnished with extra parmesan cheese and freshly ground black pepper.
